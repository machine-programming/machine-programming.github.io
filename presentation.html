Oral Presentations

Oral presentations are a key component of this course, worth 10% of your grade. They provide an opportunity to develop communication skills and share insights with your peers. Each presentation will be 25 minutes total, including ~15 minutes for the presentation and ~10 minutes for questions.

We will start the presentation from the 6th week of the course. You can sign up for a presentation slot by filling out a signup sheet. Each student can sign up for topics; each topic can be presented by at most 2 students. Students can then choose specific papers to present from the list of topics.

The presentation is graded on a completion basis. You will receive a full score if you properly present the material in class. Extra credit will be given for exceptional presentations.
